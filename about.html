About Mandi

Mandi is a qualified music teacher with experience teaching children and adults of all ages. Mandi believes that music is the greatest way you can build confidence and self-esteem, express your emotions creatively, learn fine motor skills and develop your musical ear.

She loves to teach under 5s with a gentle, encouraging approach focusing on play and exploration of sound, taking their first steps in music. Mandi is happy to support children and adults through exams with a focus on ABRSM for piano. Knowing that every learner is different, the lessons are tailored to suit everyone’s personality, pace and interests.

Mandi has played piano and sung for many years, achieving her grade 8 in Rock School vocals, a distinction with music theory, a first class degree in Music Technology and loves to compose, mix and master her music.

At the heart of it all is Mandi’s deep love of teaching — and a genuine delight in watching learners grow through music. Her goal is not just to teach notes and songs, but to nurture curiosity, confidence, and a lifelong love of making music.

1. Purpose of this Policy

This policy outlines how safeguarding is managed during private music lessons, to ensure all students, particularly children and vulnerable adults, are safe and supported during lessons.

2. My Commitment to Safeguarding

  • I am committed to providing a safe, supportive, and inclusive learning environment.
  • I recognise my responsibility to report concerns if a student may be at risk of harm.

3. Code of Conduct

  • I will behave professionally and respectfully at all times.
  • I will not share student personal information or images without written consent.
  • I will ensure all lessons are conducted in appropriate, safe environments.
  • For in-person lessons: Parents/carers are welcome to stay or be nearby.
  • For online lessons: A parent/carer must be present in the home and aware of the lesson.
  • No inappropriate language, materials, or behaviour is tolerated.

4. Reporting Concerns

  • I will raise my concern with the appropriate local safeguarding authority or service.
  • I will document the concern, date, and actions taken.
  • If a child discloses something concerning, I will listen and reassure them, but will not promise confidentiality.

5. Parent/Carer Responsibilities

  • Ensure a safe space is provided for the student during lessons.
  • Be accessible during lesson times.
  • Share relevant information (e.g. SEND, EHCPs, behaviour triggers, health needs) to allow lessons to be adapted safely.

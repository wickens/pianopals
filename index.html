Welcome to Piano Pals

Piano Pals offers a fun, nurturing environment where children can explore the joy of music through piano and singing lessons. Designed especially for younger learners, Mandi’s approach combines musical development with playful, creative expression.

Based in Basingstoke, Mandi tailors each lesson to your child’s pace and personality – building confidence and a lifelong love of music.

If your child is interested in piano lessons (suitable from age 3) or you’re looking for vocal lessons for children aged 5 and up (or even for yourself!) Mandi would love to hear what you’re after.

Please get in touch by email if you’re interested in finding out more.
